About Brea, CA

Brea, CA is a city located in northern Orange County, California, approximately 33 miles southeast of Los Angeles. As of the 2010 census, the population was 39,282, but it has grown since then to an estimated 47,325 in 2020. The city is part of the Los Angeles metropolitan area and is known for its pleasant atmosphere and strong community.

The demographic of Brea is diverse, with the following ethnic groups: White (non-Hispanic) (38.73%), Black (non-Hispanic) (2.11%), American Indian (non-Hispanic) (0.15%), and Asian (non-Hispanic) (3.56%). The city is situated in the 92821, 92823, and 92835 ZIP codes and has a land area of 12.17 square miles, with 0.04 square miles of water. The elevation of Brea is 361 feet (110 meters).

Brea is home to several points of interest, including the Brea Museum, which showcases the history and culture of the city and its surrounding area. The city is also known for its charming downtown area, which features historic buildings, shops, and restaurants. The Brea Art Gallery, located in the city's civic center, hosts rotating exhibitions of art and photography, providing a space for local artists to showcase their work. The latitude and longitude coordinates for Brea are 33.92° N, 117.9° W. The city is located in the Pacific Time Zone, observing Daylight Saving Time during the summer months.
