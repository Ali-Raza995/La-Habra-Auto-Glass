About Placentia, CA

Placentia, CA is a city located in northern Orange County, California, United States. As of the 2020 census, the population of Placentia was 51,233, with a median age of 36 and a median household income of $102,125.

The city is known for its charming downtown area, which features historic buildings, shops, and restaurants, as well as its strong community and excellent educational system. The demographic of Placentia is diverse, with the following ethnic groups: White (Non-Hispanic) (38.2%), White (Hispanic) (21.8%), Asian (Non-Hispanic) (16%), Other (Hispanic) (9.29%), and Two+ (Hispanic) (6.2%).

The city is home to several highly-rated schools, including Placentia High School, which is part of the Orange Unified School District. Some of the points of interest in Placentia include the Placentia Public Library, the Painted Park, the Placentia Art Museum, and the historic Placentia Train Depot. The city is also known for its beautiful neighborhoods, such as Old Town Placentia, which features well-preserved historic homes and a small-town atmosphere. The latitude and longitude coordinates for Placentia, CA are 33.8722° N, 117.51° W. The city is located in the Pacific Time Zone, observing Daylight Saving Time during the summer months.
