About Buena Park, CA

Buena Park, CA is a city in northern Orange County, California, United States, known for its friendly atmosphere and strong community. As of the 2020 census, the population of Buena Park was 84,034, with a median age of 36.6 and a median household income of $90,503. The city is part of the Los Angeles metropolitan area and is located in the 90620, 90623, and 92833 ZIP codes. The elevation of Buena Park is 75 feet (23 meters).

The demographic of Buena Park is diverse, with the following ethnic groups: Asian (Non-Hispanic) (32%), White (Non-Hispanic) (22.9%), White (Hispanic) (18.2%), Other (Hispanic) (11.9%), and Two+ (Hispanic) (7.02%). The city is home to 23,826 households, with an average household size of 3.36 people.

Buena Park is home to several points of interest, including the Richard Nixon Presidential Library and Museum, which offers exhibits on the life and presidency of former U.S. President Richard Nixon. The city is also known for its charming downtown area, which features historic buildings, shops, and restaurants. Knott's Berry Farm is a popular attraction in Buena Park, offering a variety of berry picking opportunities and seasonal festivals. The latitude and longitude coordinates for Buena Park are 33.868900° N, 118.012741° W. The city is located in the Pacific Time Zone, observing Daylight Saving Time during the summer months.
