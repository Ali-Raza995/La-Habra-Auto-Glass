About Tustin CA

Tustin is a diverse and vibrant city located in Orange County, California, with a population of 78,418 as of 2022. The city's demographics are characterized by a significant percentage of foreign-born residents, with 31.5% being foreign born. The population is diverse, with 21.6% Asian, 46% White, and 5% biracial or multiracial. The median household income in Tustin is $93,901, and the median property value is $735,400.

The city offers a range of points of interest, including the Tustin Ranch Golf Course, the Tustin Market Place, and a variety of parks and recreational facilities. The city's coordinates are approximately 33.7420° N latitude and 117.8236° W longitude. The latest development about the city includes a slight decline in population from 2020 to 2021, along with a growth in median household income, reflecting a dynamic and evolving community.
